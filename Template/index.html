Win Table

Wild Symbol

WILD appear on all reels
WILD substitutes for all symbols except COIN and GOLD COIN symbols

COIN SYMBOL

COIN Symbol appear on all reels

GOLD COIN SYMBOL

GOLD COIN Symbol appear on all reels
GOLD COIN Symbols may land with a value between 5x and 9x of the total Play amount

MYSTERY SYMBOL

MYSTERY symbol appears only during the bonus and can land on any vacant position on any reel.
MYSTERY Symbol can result in either MULTIPLIER or JACKPOT or THE VORTEX Feature.


Bonus Game

Get 6 or more COIN symbols in the base game to trigger the Hold For Gold bonus.

The coin symbols that trigger the bonus will carry over into the bonus game with their awarded values.

The remaining positions will be vacant for other symbols to land during the bonus game.

At the start of the bonus, the player is awarded 3 re-spins. During the re-spins, if any symbol lands on a vacant position, the re-spin count resets to 3.

The bonus ends when all re-spins are used up or all vacant positions on the game board are filled.


MYSTERY BOX

MYSTERY BOX award random prizes like multipliers, Jackpots or The Vortex.


MULTIPLIER FEATURE

A Mystery Box can award a multiplier between 1x and 9x of the Play amount that triggered the Hold For Gold feature.


JACKPOT FEATURE

A Mystery Box can award the MINI, MINOR, or MAJOR Jackpot.

Landing a Jackpot symbol can also trigger the MINI, MINOR, or MAJOR Jackpot.

Once all vacant positions are filled with symbols during the Hold For Gold feature, the GRAND Jackpot is awarded.


VORTEX FEATURE

The Mystery Box may award the VORTEX feature, which collects and awards the total value of all coin symbols on the board.


Lines

All wins award left to right on adjacent reels. Winning combinations begin with the leftmost reel. Only highest win awarded per line.


PLAY FEATURE

Selecting 'Play Feature' opens a menu where you can choose Play amount settings to purchase the feature.

After the 'Play Feature' is completed, the Play amount will remain the same as the one selected during the purchase.


TURBO SPIN MODE

Pressing Turbo button switches between NORMAL, FAST and ULTRA spin modes

FAST and ULTRA spin modes shorten the spin duration.


AUTO PLAY

Press the Auto Play button, select the number of spins, then press START to begin.

Auto Play is deactivated when a bonus or feature is triggered

Press STOP to cancel Auto Play


TIME OUT

The player session will end if the game is inactive for more than 5 minutes.
